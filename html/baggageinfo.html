Baggage


Bag types and weights

Hand Bag

Dimensions: Up to 40 x 30 x 15cm (16 x 12 x 6in)

Weights: Up to 23kg (51 lb)

More information: Hand bags can contain essentials such as medication, mobile phones, other electronic devices and passports. These must be placed under the seat and are guaranteed in the cabin.

Cabin Bag

Dimensions: Up to 56 x 45 x 25cm (22 x 18 x 10in) - includes wheels and handles

Weights: Up to 23kg (51 lb)

More information: Cabin bags shouldn't contain valuables, electronics or essential medicines. These may need to go in the hold.

Checked Baggage

Dimensions: Up to 90 x 75 x 43cm (35 x 30 x 17in)

Weights: Up to 23kg (51lb) in Economy and Premium Economy.

Up to 32kg (70lb) in First and Business.

More information: Checked in the hold.

Remember baggage allowance may vary depending on which airline you are traveling with. To be sure about baggage types and allowance check the airlines website you're flying with.
