Tourism Types

Architectural

Explore the world through its structures.

Discover iconic landmarks, ancient ruins, cutting-edge modern buildings, and the cultural stories behind them. Perfect for design lovers, history buffs, and curious travelers.

Best for: Historical cities, UNESCO sites, urban explorations.
Top Spots: Rome, Kyoto, Barcelona, Istanbul.

Gastronomic

Taste your way across the globe.

Dive into local food scenes, traditional dishes, street markets, and fine dining. Culinary experiences offer a unique window into culture.

Best for: Foodies, cooking class seekers, wine and spirits explorers.
Top Spots: Paris, Bangkok, Oaxaca, Tokyo.

Religious

Travel with spiritual purpose.

Visit sacred sites, historic places of worship, and destinations with deep spiritual meaning—whether for reflection, pilgrimage, or learning.

Best for: Pilgrims, spiritual travelers, history and culture enthusiasts.
Top Spots: Jerusalem, Mecca, Varanasi, Vatican City.

Adventure

Adrenaline meets the outdoors.

Chase thrills and explore nature through hiking, diving, ziplining, and more. Great for those seeking action, challenge, and natural beauty.

Best for: Outdoor lovers, extreme sports fans, explorers.
Top Spots: Queenstown, Patagonia, Costa Rica, Nepal.

Beaches

Relax, unwind, and soak up the sun.

Golden sands, turquoise waters, and vibrant coastal culture await. Whether it’s for surfing, snorkeling, or sipping cocktails, beaches are timeless.

Best for: Sunseekers, water sports, romantic getaways.
Top Spots: Maldives, Bali, Santorini, Cancun.
